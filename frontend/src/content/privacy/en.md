# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-17_

## Data collected

- We collect account details such as email address, password hash or OAuth identifiers, display name, avatar, subscription tier, and language preferences.
- We store search activity, saved alerts, portfolio entries, in-product conversations, and support requests that are needed to deliver the service.
- We process operational telemetry such as security logs, rate-limit counters, billing events, and error traces to keep the platform secure and available.

## Legal basis

- We process core account, billing, and product data to perform the contract we have with you.
- We retain fraud-prevention, security, and audit records where we have a legitimate interest in protecting the service and our users.
- We rely on consent before loading optional analytics scripts or other non-essential cookies.

## Retention periods

- Account and subscription records are kept while the account is active and for the period required by tax, accounting, and fraud-prevention obligations after closure.
- Alert history, portfolio activity, and application logs are retained only for the operational period needed to support the product, investigate incidents, and meet legal obligations.
- When you request account deletion, personally identifiable information is anonymised immediately and the remaining records are permanently removed within 30 days unless the law requires a longer retention period.

## Your rights

- You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
- You can ask us to correct inaccurate information.
- You can request deletion, restrict processing, object to certain processing, or ask for a portable export of your data.
- You can withdraw cookie consent at any time by clearing the consent cookie and setting your preference again.

## Cookie policy

- EstateGap uses essential cookies for authentication, session continuity, security controls, and language preferences.
- Optional analytics or performance tooling stays disabled until you consent through the cookie dialog.
- The consent cookie is stored as `eg_consent` for up to one year with `SameSite=Lax` and a site-wide path.
